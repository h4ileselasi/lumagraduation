Cap & Gown Tips

Get the most out of your graduation photos

What to Wear

Choose comfortable, formal attire that complements your gown. Light colors work best under dark gowns. Avoid busy patterns.

Cap Placement

Wear your cap flat on your head, about an inch above your eyebrows. Use bobby pins to secure it properly.

Timing

Schedule your session 2-3 weeks before graduation to avoid last-minute rush and ensure availability.

Posing Tips

Relax your shoulders, stand tall, and hold your diploma with confidence. Natural smiles work best!

Lighting

Schedule outdoor sessions during golden hour (early morning or late afternoon) for the best natural light.

Location Ideas

Choose meaningful locations like your faculty building, library, or favorite campus spot for personal touch.
